Who is Borussia Dortmund?

Borussia Dortmund is a german football club. The club plays in the first division in Germany. Borussia Dortmund was invited in 1909. The colours of the club are black and yellow. Borussia Dortmund is a traditional football club and plays in the Bundesliga since 50 Years. In the all time table of the Bundesliga Borussia Dortmund is second. They won 8 times the Bundesliga Titel and 5 times the german cup.

Borussia Dortmund biggest success!

Borussia Dortmund won 8 german Bundesligas. So they won the third most Bundesliga in the history. The last time they won the Bundesliga was 2012. They also won 5 german cups so they won also the third most titles. The last time Borussia Dortmund won the german cup was 2021. 1997 Borussia Dortmund won the Champions League. The Champions League is the biggest Trophy in the European Football and it is the biggest success in the history of Borussia Dortmund.

Signal Iduna Park

The name of the stadium in Dortmund is Signal Iduna Park. The Signal Iduna Park is the biggest Stadium in Germany. It has a capacity of 81 365 places. The seats have the color black and yellow like the club colors. Not only Borussia Dortmund plays in the Signal Iduna Park, also the Germany national team plays there and the stadium will also be part of the European championship 2024 which will be host in Germany.
